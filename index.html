Mission

Brick & Bloom was started with a simple belief: Triathlon is for every body.

For many of us, triathlon isn't about the finish line. It's about the Tuesday morning swim that clears the static in our heads. It's about the bike ride where nobody needs us for a few hours. We don’t train to escape, we train to build the strength to endure life better.

We focus on the Brick of challenging training and the Bloom personal growth that follows.

This is a Santa Rosa community designed to lower the barrier to entry, by fostering connections and providing resources.

No gatekeeping. No dues. Start where you are. Use what you have. Do what you can.

About

Brick & Bloom was started by a 40-something single mom. She was inspired by her mother who registered for her first tri before she had a bike to find a different source of strength after her transition to single motherhood.

The founder learned to swim just to finish her first local sprint tri in a similar season of life. She believes triathlon isn't just a sport. It was a way to stop being everything to everyone and start being something to herself.

Whether you are in a season of Brick or Bloom she want there to be a place for every body in the sport.
