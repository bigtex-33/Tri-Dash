Our Mission

Brick & Bloom was founded on a simple truth: Triathlon is for every body.

In the shadow of traditional "Iron" culture, many athletes—particularly women and minorities—feel there isn't a seat at the table. We are here to change that.

We focus on the Brick (the hard work of training) and the Bloom (the personal growth that follows). This is a Santa Rosa collective designed to lower the barrier to entry.

No gatekeeping. No dues. Just movement.
