Mission

Brick & Bloom was started with a simple belief: Triathlon is for every body.

Many athletes, particularly women and people of color, feel there isn't space to support them in getting started.

We focus on the Brick of challenging training and the Bloom personal growth that follows. This is a Santa Rosa community designed to lower the barrier to entry, by fostering connections and providing resources.

No gatekeeping. No dues. Just movement.

About

Brick & Bloom was started by a 40-something single mom after experiencing the gap in triathlon resources in Santa Rosa. Inspired by her mother who registered for her first tri before she had a bike, the founder learned to swim just to finish her first local tri. We believe triathlon isn't just a sport. It's a sanctuary.

Whether you are in a season of Brick or Bloom there is a place for you here.

Brick & Bloom is aimed to help you start. Everyone is welcome. Start where you are. Use what you have. Do what you can.
