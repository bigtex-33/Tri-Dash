Our Mission

Brick & Bloom was founded on a simple truth: Triathlon is for every body.

Many athletes—particularly women and minorities—feel the high cost, high struggle barriers to trying triathlons. We are here to change that.

We focus on the Brick hard work of training and the Bloom personal growth that follows.

We offer space to find resources and connect designed to lower the barrier to entry.

No gatekeeping. No dues. Just movement.

About

Brick & Bloom was started by Lauren after experiencing the gap in triathlon resources in Santa Rosa.

She is a second generation single-mom, who turned to triathlon during a difficult chapter. Like her mother, she's found the challenge empowring and regulating at times when other outlets didn't work.

Lauren sought out a community between starting and seeking out coaching, and couldn't find one. The post-COVID landscape was different (non-existent) for local traithlon clubs.

As she's discovered resources for inexpensive gear, local clubs and outings that are low cost, and sought community she realized that this may be what others are looking for as well.

Brick & Bloomis aimed to help you start. No gatekeeping. No dues. Just movement.
